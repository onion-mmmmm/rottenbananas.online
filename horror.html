Horror

The Exorcist (1973)

Rating: ★★★★★

The Exorcist is a landmark horror film that masterfully blends psychological depth with supernatural terror. Centered on a young girl’s demonic possession and two priests’ desperate fight to save her, it features chilling performances and an unsettling atmosphere. Iconic effects and haunting sound design make it a timeless benchmark of fear.

The Shining (1980)

Rating: ★★★★★

The Shining is a masterful psychological horror film directed by Stanley Kubrick, featuring Jack Nicholson’s unforgettable portrayal of a writer spiraling into madness. Set in the eerie Overlook Hotel, the film’s haunting visuals, unsettling score, and sense of isolation create a deeply unnerving experience. Kubrick’s meticulous direction and the film’s layers of ambiguity make it a timeless classic.

A Nightmare on Elm Street (1984)

Rating: ★★★★☆

Wes Craven’s A Nightmare on Elm Street redefined the slasher genre with its terrifying premise of a killer who haunts and murders teens in their dreams. Robert Englund’s Freddy Krueger blends menace and dark humor, making him an unforgettable villain. With surreal dream sequences and a clever twist ending, the film remains a cult classic and a standout in horror history.

Get Out (2017)

Rating: ★★★★★

*Jordan Peele’s Get Out* is a brilliant blend of social commentary and psychological horror, exploring race relations in modern America. The film follows Chris, a Black man uncovering a chilling secret at his white girlfriend’s family estate. With exceptional performances and a gripping storyline, Peele’s film is both unsettling and thought-provoking, cementing its place as a modern horror masterpiece.

Hereditary (2018)

Rating: ★★★★★

Hereditary is a modern horror film that blends psychological drama with supernatural terror, focusing on the Graham family's unraveling after a series of tragedies. Directed by Ari Aster, the film explores grief, trauma, and family dynamics, with Toni Collette delivering a career-defining performance. Filled with disturbing imagery and a chilling final act, *Hereditary* is a standout horror film that lingers long after viewing.
