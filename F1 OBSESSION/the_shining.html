The Shining (1980)

Rating: ★★★★★

The Shining is a masterful psychological horror film directed by Stanley Kubrick, featuring Jack Nicholson’s unforgettable portrayal of a writer spiraling into madness. Set in the eerie Overlook Hotel, the film’s haunting visuals, unsettling score, and sense of isolation create a deeply unnerving experience. Kubrick’s meticulous direction and the film’s layers of ambiguity make it a timeless classic.
